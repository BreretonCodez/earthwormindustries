How We Work

Kentucky Farm Control is a farm management system that allows users to monitor and store their crop yield, livestock, chemicals, and equipment owned and produced by the farm. This places the farmer at an extreme advantage as he can manage his farm and assets, even when he is not in the field, creating an even more productive and efficient farm.






LiveStock Tracking

Track and monitor the population of your livestock as you sell, breed and buy your animals.


Crop Tracking

Track and monitor the amount of crops you have as you sell, grow and harvest your crops.


Smart Irrigation

Automate and manage the watering of your crops.


Chemical Tracking

Track the use of fertilizers and pesticides on your crops and plantations.


Equipment Management

Manage your farm's mechanical equipment and tools inventory.


Logistics

Manage your farm's business affairs. Pay your workers, send invoices, get paid, fulfil purchase orders, invoices, and manage wholesale, retail and market trips.
